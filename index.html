Joshua Mario Jude Bernadel

One of the few people whose birthday I can remember without a Facebook reminder.

Timeline

  1. Nov·09·1998

    06:30 a.m. | Hackensack, NJ

    Josh was born and the Bernadel family grew by one. Laurie was no longer an only child and was ecstatic about finally meeting the baby brother to whom she regularly sang "My Little Sunshine" through her mom's stomach.

  2. Aug·04·2003

    Mooresville, NC

    The Bernadels packed up their cars and moved south to North Carolina. This is where Josh started pre-K, beginning his trend to be recognized anywhere in town.

  3. 2004 - 2013
    Elementary & Middle School

    Josh captured the hearts of many teachers and the school bus drivers. He knew all the bus numbers and their routes. During this time, he also started to develop an affinity for swimming and cross-country running.

  4. 06·30·2014
    Heart Surgery

    Levine Children's Hospital

    One of the biggest moments in Josh's life was when he underwent open heart surgery to fix a congenital heart valve defect. With a massive support system, he remained positive during the entire process.

  5. 06·10·2017
    High School Graduation

    Josh graduated from high school with pride, ready to take on the world. By this point, he already had 4 different jobs, a bustling social life and participated in various sports.

  6. Aug·2017
    College Beginnings

    Columbus, OH

    Josh began his undergraduate journey at The Ohio State University, studying Supply Chain and Logistics within their top business program. Class of 2021! Go Bucks!

  7. Summer 2019
    Williams-Sonoma Internship

    Memphis, TN

    After many interviews, Josh got accepted to intern with Williams-Sonoma. He spent the entire summer there, learning more about his business specialty and presented his project to their CEO; it saved the company $100k!

  8. Nov·09·2019
    Josh turned 21 today!

    It's official! Josh is now a full adult. He's fully licensed to party, no questions asked and no fake ID needed. Being this free comes with great responsibility.

There's so much more in store for Josh. He's got a bright future!
